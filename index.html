About

Welcome. My name is Dennis Oliver Kubitza, 27 years young, PhD Student at the Bundesinstitut für Berufsbildung (Federal Institute on Vocational Education). Previously I was working in the research area of Data Integration through Semantic Web Technologies and in the conceptualization and creation of large scale decentralized Data Spaces (Gaia-X and International Data Spaces). Projects I have been involved (Project Management and Software Engineering) are the Ministry funded LIMBO Project, Mobility Data Spaces and the upcoming Datenraum Mobilitaet . In addition, I have worked on Data Integration for Autonomous Vehicles. This was done in cooperation with a big international car manufacturer. Despite my current professional work, my key areas of interest are:
  • Non-parametric Statistics and Stochastic Modelling
  • Behavioural Economics and Social Studies
  • Large Data Analysis and Architectures
In November 2018 I attended the Fraunhofer Data Scientist Training for Big Data Architectures. Since November 2020 I am allowed to call myself Certified Data Scientist Specialized in Trustworthy AI.

Projects

The Semangit Project

SemanGit is the first collection of linked data extracted from GitHub based on a git ontology we designed and extended to include specific GitHub features. The large scale datasets consists of roughly 20 000 000 000 data triples and can be used for the analysis of individual relations between entities. Showcase Analysis of the Dataset cover the cooperation of countries in international organisations, the social structures within programming teams and a regional based analysis of the usage of programming languages. The dataset and Use Cases were presented with two publications at the International Semantic Web Conference 2019, Auckland.
