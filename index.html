About

Welcome to my Homepage. My name is Dennis Oliver Kubitza, 27 years young, research Engineer and Project Manager at Fraunhofer IAIS. Currently I am working in the field of Data Integration in the context of Semantic Web Technologies and in large scale dezentralized Data Spaces (Gaia-X and International Data Spaces), both in the context of Mobility. Recent projects I have been involved (management and Coding) are the publicly funded LIMBO Project, Mobility Data Spaces and the upcoming Datenraum Mobilitaet , where Fraunhofer IAIS is providing Software for Infrastrucutre Compoenents. In the business area I have worked on Data Integration for Autonomous Vehicles in cooperation with a big international car manufracturer.
  • Non-parametric Statistics and Stochastic Modelling
  • Behavioural Economics, Game Theory and Social Studies
  • Big Data Applications and Data Science
In November 2018 I attended the Fraunhofer Data Scientist Training for Big Data Architectures. Since November 2020 I own a Certification as Data Scientist Specialized in Trustworthy AI .
